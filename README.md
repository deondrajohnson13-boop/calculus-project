# calculus-project
This honors Statistic project analyzed why students left VCentials, at Valencia College, without taking items. I helped collect data, organize survey responses and compare Spring vs. Fall results. Our findings showed higher traffic and slightly more people were leaving without taking any items in Fall 2025.
